## Overview

The **UNCC Student Coalition** is a student-led organization dedicated to empowering youth and building a network focused on organizing and supporting local communities for **racial, social, and climate justice**. This website serves as the central hub for our coalition, advertising our mission, highlighting our campaigns, and providing avenues for students to get involved and collectively organize.

-----
Live page: https://webpages.charlotte.edu/dpham17/Project-official/index.html

## âœ¨ Our Mission & Vision

The core purpose of the UNCC Student Coalition is to provide a unified voice for students, especially in light of recent crises like the COVID-19 pandemic, where student input on university decisions has been lacking.

  * **Empowerment:** To empower students to have a greater voice in all aspects of their lives and in the decisions made by UNCC.
  * **Organization:** To be a space where students can collectively organize and support their communities through various social movements.
  * **Development:** To offer **training opportunities**, **leadership development programs**, and community-oriented skill building.
  * **Advocacy:** To emulate successful student-led organizations advocating for a fairer and more just world.

## ðŸ›  Technology & Dependencies

The project utilizes standard front-end web technologies along with specific libraries for enhanced functionality:

  * **HTML:** Structuring the website content, including semantic HTML forms.
  * **CSS:** Styling and visual presentation.
  * **JavaScript:** For interactivity and dynamic features.
