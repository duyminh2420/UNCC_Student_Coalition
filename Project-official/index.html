About Us

The UNCC Student Coalition is a student lead organization focusing on organizing and supporting our local communities for movements such as racial, social, and climate justice. We offer training opportunities, leadership development programs, and an abundance of other community orientated skills. Our organization strives to emulate other successful student led organizations that have advocated for a fairer and more just world.

Our mission

The UNCC Student Coalition is a student lead organization that is dedicated to empowering and creating a network of youths working to improve their futures. We focus on allowing students to get together to organize and support their communities for movements such as racial, social, and climate justice.

Frenquently asked questions

How do I join a club on campus?

Sign up the form we provide in new member section and follow up our lastest news and meetings in your emails. Certain clubs also have additional requirements for active membership.

How do I start a new club on campus?

Starting a club on campus is a process that involves a few steps. On the Center for Student Engagement’s Starting a New Club page, you will find all of the necessary steps to starting your very own club. Know that this process can take two or more weeks to complete by contact us.

Who is my Club Commissioner?

Your Commissioner’s contact information, biography, and goals for the year can be found by clicking on the pictures on our NinerEngage Page by clicking to contact us. Your Commissioner is the person assigned to be a liaison for your club coalition.
